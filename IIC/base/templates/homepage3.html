About IIC

Cultivating Innovation Across Higher Education

Systematically fostering a culture of innovation across all Higher Education Institutions

MoE's Innovation Cell (MIC)

The Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, has established the MoE's Innovation Cell (MIC) to systematically foster a culture of innovation across all Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). The primary objective of MIC is to encourage, inspire, and nurture young students by supporting them in developing new ideas and transforming those ideas into viable prototypes.

Institution's Innovation Councils (IICs)

As part of its vision, MIC initiated the creation of Institution's Innovation Councils (IICs) in selected HEIs. A network of these IICs has been formed to promote innovation within institutions through diverse activities, thereby creating a vibrant innovation ecosystem on campuses.

Core Purpose

The core purpose of this Cell is to cultivate creativity and design thinking in various fields of engineering, with an emphasis on aesthetic and practical approaches. The Innovation Cell provides students with opportunities to showcase their talents, collaborate in teams, and participate in competitions. It also serves as a platform to help students excel in their specialized areas of interest while encouraging young, talented minds to explore and realize their full potential.

Our Vision

Transforming into a Leading Innovation Hub

The Vision of the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) is to transform the Institute into a leading hub of innovation, creativity, entrepreneurship, and start-up culture, where students and faculty are continuously encouraged, empowered, and equipped to translate ideas into impactful solutions for society, industry, and the nation.
